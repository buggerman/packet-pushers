Packet Pushers is the ultimate browser-based drug trading simulation game inspired by the classic Dope Wars. Experience the thrill of street commerce in a nostalgic 80s terminal interface.

Game Features:

  • Weapons shop with unique combat system

  • Random events and police encounters
